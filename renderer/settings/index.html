Checking for Spotify…

No login, no Spotify account linking — this talks directly to the local Spotify app through Windows' built-in media session system, the same one your keyboard's media keys use. Just have Spotify open and these hotkeys work anywhere, on Free or Premium. Volume hotkeys control ONLY Spotify's volume, leaving your main system volume untouched.
